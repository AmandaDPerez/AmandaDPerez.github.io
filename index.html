Health Equity Researcher & Psychology Instructor · California · adp@berkeley.edu

Welcome to my curated Github page. I am a Post-Doctoral Researcher at UC Berkeley's School of Public Health. I am experienced in quantitative methods with a background in social psychology. R is my primary statistical software.

Apart from my work, I enjoy spending time with my pets, building legos, cooking, and crocheting. When forced outdoors, I like to visit botanical gardens and try new vegan restaurants.
